Food Carts in Portland

One of the most popular ways to eat in Portland are food carts.
The food is fresh, cheap, and there are frequently multiple options in one area.
Here is a map of food carts in Portland!

  • Bulkogi Korean BBQ
  • The Swamp Shack
  • Francisco Taqueria

A few food carts in East Portland are:

  • El Brasero
  • Fried Egg I'm In Love
